Our Story

Five Years of Weaving Trust with Retailers Across India

Shree Vastra was founded in 2019 with a single purpose: to make premium ethnic wear accessible to retailers, boutique owners, and resellers at honest wholesale prices — without the usual middlemen and uncertainty.

Starting from a small warehouse in Delhi's Chandni Chowk, we've grown into a trusted wholesale partner for over a thousand retail businesses across India. From small-town boutiques in Rajasthan to fashion stores in metro cities, our customers trust us because we deliver what we promise — consistently, on time, and at the right price.

Our quality commitment is simple: every saree, suit, and dupatta leaving our warehouse has been checked by hand. We work directly with weavers in Varanasi, Surat, Jaipur, and Kolkata, which means you get authentic products at fair prices and we can guarantee stock availability year-round.
